### FAQ Section

**Q1: What is Aveo?**
Aveo is an AI platform that lets you create character brands and automatically produce publishable short form videos at scale. You define your character's look, voice, and personality. Aveo handles everything else, from scriptwriting to video production to publishing on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

**Q2: Do I need any video editing skills?**
No. Aveo handles the entire production pipeline automatically. Scripts, voiceover, lip sync, and final compositing all happen without any manual editing. You can review and tweak scripts before production, but editing software is never required.

**Q3: What platforms do you publish to?**
Aveo supports TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels. Starter plan users download and upload manually. Growth plan users get auto publishing to 2 platforms. Pro plan users get unlimited auto publishing across all supported platforms.

**Q4: How long does it take to create a video?**
Your first character can be set up in under 10 minutes using our guided wizard. After that, video production happens automatically. Most videos render within 30 to 60 minutes, depending on complexity. With auto publishing, videos go live on schedule without any manual steps.

**Q5: Can I use my own voice?**
Pro plan users can clone their own voice or any voice they have legal rights to use. Growth plan users choose from a library of 20+ professional voices. All plans include emotion and pacing controls to fine tune how your character sounds.

**Q6: Is the content actually good enough to publish?**
Yes. Aveo's script engine is trained on high performing short form content across dozens of niches. Our users report 2 to 3x higher engagement rates compared to their previous manually produced content. You always have the option to review and edit scripts before production.

**Q7: Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. All plans are month to month with no long term contracts. You can cancel, upgrade, or downgrade at any time. Annual plans offer a 20% discount but can still be cancelled with a prorated refund within the first 30 days.

**Q8: What happens if I hit my video limit?**
You will receive a notification when you reach 80% of your monthly video limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your capacity. Unused videos do not roll over to the next month, so choose the plan that matches your content needs.

### Pricing FAQ

**Q1: What happens after my free trial ends?**
Your 14 day free trial gives you full access to the Growth plan. When it ends, you can choose any plan to continue, or your account will be paused with no charges. You keep all your characters and content.

**Q2: Can I change plans later?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Your content is never deleted.

**Q3: What counts as a "video"?**
Each completed video that goes through our production pipeline counts as one video. Draft scripts and failed renders do not count toward your limit. Re renders of the same script also do not count extra.

**Q4: Do you offer annual pricing?**
Yes. Annual plans come with a 20% discount, saving you between $190 and $1,190 per year depending on your plan. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise pricing with volume discounts.

**Q5: Can I use Aveo for client work?**
Absolutely. Many agencies use Aveo Pro to manage content for multiple clients. Each character can be configured for a different client or brand. Pro plan includes custom branding so your clients see your agency's work.

**Q6: What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and ACH bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can request invoicing with net 30 terms.
